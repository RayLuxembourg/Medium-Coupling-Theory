# Torus Parameters: Determining the Medium's Geometry from Observation

*Status: In development*

This document addresses Open Problem 5 from the [main framework](mathematical-framework.md#15-summary). The fundamental parameters of the universe in MCT are $R_T$ (major radius), $a_T$ (minor radius), $\Gamma_p$ (poloidal circulation), and $\Gamma_t$ (toroidal circulation). The goal is to constrain these from existing observational data.
