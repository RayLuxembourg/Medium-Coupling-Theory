# Strong and Weak Nuclear Forces as Medium Coupling Modes

*Status: In development*

This document addresses Open Problem 4 from the [main framework](mathematical-framework.md#15-summary). MCT accounts for gravity (poloidal coupling) and electromagnetism (a second flow mode, see [Kaluza-Klein connection](kaluza-klein.md)). The strong and weak nuclear forces remain. Are they additional medium coupling modes, or do they emerge from the existing structure at short distances?
