# Kaluza-Klein Connection: Unifying Medium Coupling Modes

*Status: In development*

This document addresses Open Problem 3 from the [main framework](mathematical-framework.md#15-summary). The medium carries at least two coupling modes (gravitational and electromagnetic, see [Section 8.7](mathematical-framework.md#87-connection-to-electromagnetism-in-mct)). The goal is to formalize this two-mode structure and connect it to Kaluza-Klein theory, where gravity and electromagnetism unify through an extra spatial dimension.
